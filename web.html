Small, wireless and scalable device to automate and log your OD600 measurements of bacteria or yeast.

Click, to stay in touch.

Why you'll love ODee.

Wireless

ODee clips on to an ODee conical flask or 15mL falcon tube and automatically measures the OD600 every 15 seconds. It is small and light weight so does not affect your shaker.

Real-time

ODee beams the OD600 measurements to the cloud for you to access in real time on your laptop or smartphone. You can also set customised alerts to let you know when your culture is ready for its supplements.

Works with you

ODee fits into your existing set-up and routine so you don't need to go out and buy a new incubator. All you need is the ODee clip and conical flask and your set for doing up to 4 parallel ODee measurements at once!
